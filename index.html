About Pavothil Temple

Check our story

Pavothil temple, around 300+ years old temple with Bhagvan Mahadeva as the main diety, then as Upa devathas Ganapathy, Ayyappa Swamy, Durga Devi, Badra kali Devi, Vettaikkaran, Hanuman Swamy, Brahmarakshas and Naga yakshi. Also are present Nagangal and vadakkan Chovva bagavathi in sarppa kavu.
Everyday pooja and dhaara for Mahadevan and abhishekam nivedhyam archana for Upa devatha. Pradosha Pooja twice in a month. Every month Ayilya pooja and annual special Tantri pooja for Nagangal (Ameda Naga Raja Temple Tantri) and Ayyappan pattu, Tantri Pooja on punartha day of Medam.
